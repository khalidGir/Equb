# E-Qub User Personas

## Persona 1: Kebede - The Traditional Administrator

**Demographics:**
- Age: 42
- Location: Addis Ababa
- Occupation: Government Office Manager
- Education: Bachelor's Degree
- Tech Savviness: Intermediate
- Income Level: Middle Class

**Background:**
Kebede has been managing his office eQub group for over 5 years with traditional methods using notebooks and phone calls. He is respected among his peers and has a good reputation for honesty and organizational skills. However, he struggles with keeping accurate records and ensuring timely contributions from all members.

**Goals:**
- Simplify the record-keeping process
- Increase transparency for all group members
- Reduce time spent on administrative tasks
- Maintain his leadership position in the group

**Challenges:**
- Members sometimes forget to pay or dispute records
- Manual calculations are prone to errors
- Difficult to track payments when members travel
- Time-consuming to collect and distribute funds

**Technology Usage:**
- Uses smartphone daily for calls, messaging, and basic apps
- Comfortable with WhatsApp and phone banking
- Hesitant to adopt completely new technologies
- Prefers solutions that are simple and straightforward

## Persona 2: Aster - The Modern Participant

**Demographics:**
- Age: 29
- Location: Addis Ababa
- Occupation: Freelance Graphic Designer
- Education: Bachelor's Degree
- Tech Savviness: High
- Income Level: Variable/Freelance

**Background:**
Aster participates in multiple eQubs - one with her colleagues, one with university friends, and one with neighbors. As a freelancer, her income varies monthly, and she often travels for work. She finds it challenging to keep up with all the different groups and their schedules.

**Goals:**
- Easily track all her eQub commitments in one place
- Receive timely notifications about payment due dates
- Participate in groups regardless of her physical location
- Have transparent access to group financial records

**Challenges:**
- Juggling multiple eQub groups and schedules
- Difficulty participating when traveling or busy with work
- Wanting assurance that her payments are recorded correctly
- Need for flexible payment options

**Technology Usage:**
- Highly comfortable with smartphones and apps
- Uses digital payment platforms regularly
- Active on social media and messaging apps
- Quick to adopt new technologies that solve real problems

## Persona 3: Mahmoud - The Overseas Contributor

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35
- Location: Dubai (originally from Addis Ababa)
- Occupation: Construction Project Manager
- Education: Diploma in Civil Engineering
- Tech Savviness: Basic to Intermediate
- Income Level: High

**Background:**
Mahmoud is part of a family eQub group back in Addis Ababa. He contributes regularly but faces difficulties staying connected with the group activities due to distance. He sends money via hawala systems and relies on family members to confirm receipt and update him on group activities.

**Goals:**
- Stay connected with his home eQub despite geographical distance
- Make secure contributions without relying on intermediaries
- Access real-time updates on group activities and financial status
- Participate in group decisions and discussions remotely

**Challenges:**
- Dependence on family members to communicate group updates
- Uncertainty about whether contributions are properly recorded
- Difficulty participating in group discussions and decisions
- Concerns about transparency and accountability from afar

**Technology Usage:**
- Uses smartphone primarily for calls and WhatsApp
- Relies on family members for help with complex apps
- Comfortable with mobile money transfers
- Prefers simple, trustworthy solutions with clear benefits
