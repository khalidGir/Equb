This document lists primary personas for the Ethiopian E-Q-U-B digital savings application.

## 1) Administrator (App Admin)
- Role: System administrator and platform manager.
- Background: Tech-savvy staff at the microfinance or community organization that maintains the E-Q-U-B app and its user base.
- Goals:
  - Ensure uptime, backups, and security.
  - Manage group creation, policy enforcement, and dispute resolution.
  - Monitor transaction metrics and generate reports for stakeholders.
- Responsibilities:
  - Create and deactivate groups and roles.
  - Manage user accounts and authentication (password resets, MFA enforcement).
  - Oversee audit logs and authorize high-privilege operations.
- Pain Points:
  - Ensuring data privacy and regulatory compliance.
  - Handling fraudulent or duplicate accounts.
- Typical Tasks in App:
  - Review group activity dashboard.
  - Export financial reports (CSV/PDF).
  - Configure onboarding flows for new members.

## 2) Treasurer (Group Treasurer)
- Role: Financial steward for an individual eQub group.
- Background: A trusted member elected by the group to collect contributions, record payments, and distribute payouts.
- Goals:
  - Accurately record member contributions and disburse payouts on schedule.
  - Keep transparent records for group members.
  - Minimize manual reconciliation work.
- Responsibilities:
  - Record payments received from members (cash or transfer).
  - Schedule payouts according to the rotation and confirm receipts.
  - Share balance statements and transaction history with the group.
- Pain Points:
  - Reconciling payments when members pay late or via different channels.
  - Avoiding disputes over missing or double-recorded contributions.
- Typical Tasks in App:
  - Enter or confirm member payments.
  - Mark payouts as paid and attach receipts.
  - Generate a distribution schedule and balance sheet for the next meeting.

## 3) Regular Member (Participant)
- Role: Typical member of an eQub group who contributes savings and receives payouts.
- Background: Everyday urban or peri-urban Ethiopian worker who uses a mobile phone (feature phone or smartphone), may have varying literacy and tech comfort.
- Goals:
  - Pay contributions on time and receive expected payouts.
  - Track personal contribution history and upcoming payout dates.
  - Keep financial participation transparent and trust-based.
- Responsibilities:
  - Submit contributions when due (in-person cash or mobile transfer).
  - Verify their balance and confirm receipt of payouts.
- Pain Points:
  - Limited or expensive mobile data connectivity.
  - Unclear transaction receipts when payments are made outside the app.
- Typical Tasks in App:
  - View contribution history and group summary.
  - Submit proof of payment (photo, transaction code) and get confirmation.
  - Receive push/SMS reminders for upcoming contributions.

---

These personas can be extended with additional roles such as Auditor, Group Organizer, or External Financial Partner. They should be used to guide UI decisions, access control, and feature priorities.
